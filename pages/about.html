About Us

Sarthak ek independent informational platform hai jiska uddeshya users ko Sarkari Yojana, Government Jobs, Scholarships, Documents aur Online Apply Process se judi sahi, updated aur verified jankari provide karna hai.

Hum kisi bhi government department ya agency se directly connected nahi hain. Hum kisi bhi scheme, job ya certificate ke liye application process ya paise lene ka kaam nahi karte.

Sarthak ka goal hai ki users ko official government websites tak sahi guidance ke saath pahunchaya ja sake, taaki wo fraud, fake schemes aur galat information se bach sakein.

Sarthak is an independent informational platform that provides accurate and updated information related to government schemes, government jobs, scholarships, official documents, and online application procedures.

We are not affiliated with any government department or authority. We do not process applications, collect fees, or represent any government organization.

Our objective is to guide users to official government portals and help them understand the correct application process while staying safe from fake schemes and misleading information.
