IPO (Initial Public Offering) ka matlab hota hai jab koi company pehli baar stock market me apne shares public ke liye issue karti hai.

IPO kya hota hai?

IPO ke through company public investors se paisa raise karti hai. Retail investors IPO me apply karke listing ke din profit kama sakte hain, lekin risk bhi hota hai.

IPO kaise apply karein?

  • Valid Demat Account hona chahiye
  • Trading account (Zerodha, Groww, Angel One, etc.)
  • UPI linked bank account

IPO apply karne ka process

  1. Trading app ya bank ASBA section open karein
  2. Active IPO select karein
  3. Lots & price choose karein
  4. UPI mandate approve karein

IPO allotment kaise check karein?

IPO allotment registrar websites jaise Link Intime aur KFintech par check kiya jata hai.

Important Note

IPO investment market risk ke subject hote hain. Apply karne se pehle company ka prospectus zarur padhein.
